Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art known for its dynamic kicks, fast footwork, and disciplined techniques. The name “Taekwondo” roughly translates to “the way of the foot and hand.” It combines combat and self-defense skills with sport and philosophy, emphasizing respect, integrity, perseverance, and self-control.
